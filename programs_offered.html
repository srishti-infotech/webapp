Admission Criteria :


Admission will be given on first come first serve basis if the seats are full in one centre the name of the child will be kept in wait list & if any vacancies arise the school authorities will in form the Parents. If not parents will be referred to another closest SCRIBBLE PRESCHOOL Centre.


Admission procedure:


  1. Parents should visit scribble centre with their ward for the overview of school & understanding the importance of Preschool & Benefits with Scribble.
  2. Collection of Admission Kit :
  3. Submission of Admission kit within mandatory document copies.

    • Birth Certificate
    • ID Proof of Parents : (copy of PAN / license / passport )
    • Recent Passport photo of (2 copies)
    • Recent stamp size photo of parents (2 copies)
    • Passport photo of parents (2 each)
    • Passport photo nominee (1 each)
  4. Payment of fees and collecting startup kit
