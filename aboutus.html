About Us :-


CLIQUE INNOVATIONS



Clique Innovations was established in year 2008 by a group of professionals dedicated to develop and provide systematic academic and nonacademic development program based on extensive researches & self evaluation systems to enhance every child’s horizon, so that they emerge as smart, happy, caring individuals with high moral values.


Scribble Pre School :



A brainchild of ‘CLIQUE INNOVATIONS’ is a chain of child development centers. It strives to create a strong foundation for children aged 18 months to 6 years through structured “MSMG (Minimum Strain Maximum Gain) Curriculum” incorporated with age appropriate and fun filled activities backed by various reporting systems to ensure individual care and attention for every child. It ensures to provide development opportunities to sustain the competitive world for every child it offers “EVENING HOBBY CLUB” which also acts as the stress buster for children with hectic routine along with providing a platform to develop and upgrade their skills.



Scribble preschool – An experience of lifetime, the place your child feels he/she belongs too,

-


At Scribble it’s a firm belief that the best performance comes from a happy individual similarly the best learning happens in an entertaining and comfortable environment. All Scribble centers are structured for all round development of child.


Appropriate teaching aids: We use appropriate non toxic and absolutely safe toys and other equipment which enhance the child’s abilities and make the whole experience participating & entertaining.


Architecture: all centers are designed for children and are very child friendly, entertaining , interesting , colorful , with sand pits, garden, kitchen garden, sand pits, colorful theme walls, art corners , cheerful and comfortable modern furniture, fun room , audio visual room and much more…


Systematic approach: we follow day wise curriculum which is a mix of fun filled activities, interactive learning with toys and games, role plays and puppet shows and much more, which is followed by daily reporting structure which ensures attention on every child one on one.
